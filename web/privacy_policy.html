Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2023-10-27

Welcome to Wassistant. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Wassistant is designed to prioritize your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to our servers.

  • Phone Numbers & Messages: Any phone numbers or messages you enter into the app are processed locally on your device to generate links or QR codes. They are not sent to any backend server managed by us.
  • QR Codes: QR codes are generated locally using device resources.
  • vCards: Contact information used to generate vCards is processed locally.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect data, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to provide personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app stability and performance.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet: To load advertisements and open WhatsApp links.
  • Storage/Gallery: To save generated QR codes to your device.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
