Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

WAssistant ("we", "our", or "us") provides WhatsApp utility tools including link and QR code generation. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Data Collection

Offline First: WAssistant is designed to operate primarily on your device. We do not collect or store the phone numbers or messages you enter into our tools on our servers.

Device Data: We use Firebase Analytics and Sentry to collect anonymous technical data (device model, crash logs, and feature usage counts) to improve the application's performance and stability.

2. Permissions

Camera: Used strictly for scanning phone numbers via On-Device OCR (Machine Learning). Images are processed locally and are never uploaded.

Clipboard: Used only when you explicitly paste or use the auto-detection feature to help you enter phone numbers faster.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with Google AdMob for advertising. Google may use advertising IDs to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
