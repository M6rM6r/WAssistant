Frequently Asked Questions

Get help with using Wassistant

Is my phone number saved? +
No. We prioritize your privacy. All phone numbers and messages are processed locally on your device to generate the WhatsApp link. Nothing is sent to our servers.
Does this work without saving the contact? +
Yes! That is the main feature. You can enter any phone number and start a chat immediately without adding them to your address book.
How do I generate a QR code? +
Simply enter your number and an optional message in the app, then switch to the "QR Code" tab or click the QR icon. You can then save or share the image.
Is this app official? +
No, Wassistant is a third-party tool developed to enhance your WhatsApp experience. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by WhatsApp Inc.
